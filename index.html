Our Story

Walk with us through what became the best part of our lives.

  • First We Meet

    December 31st, 2019

    Our story began unexpectedly on the 31st of December 2020, during a hilarious Twitter thread that had us both laughing out loud and, unknowingly, inching closer together. What started as witty replies and playful banter soon blossomed into something neither of us anticipated—a connection that felt like fate

  • Our First Date

    January 14, 2020

    Our first date, on the 14th of January 2021, at Alyce-Ice Creamrey and Confectionaries, remains one of our fondest memories. Sharing scoops of joy and endless laughter eventually sprinkled with toppings Anxiety for Mathew as he decided the moment was "now or never", but despite the heavily tensed aura and some really sweaty palms, this was the moment we knew we had something truly special.

  • In a Relationship

    January 14, 2020

    After weeks of conversations and growing fondness, we began dating on the 14th of January 2021, even though as at said date, Mathew was unaware he was in a relationship as it existed only in Toriah's haed. This twist was due to the fact that on this day, Mathew’s intensions were made known & Toriah being Toriah accepted in her head but wasn't going to let him off that easy, leading to some extra days on the edge for Mathew but despite Mathew’s busy schedule as a producer in a bustling radio house, we found ways to nurture our bond.

The journey since has been nothing short of amazing,
and we can’t wait to continue building our forever.
